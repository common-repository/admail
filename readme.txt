AdMail is a WooCommerce extension that enables your customers to subscribe to out-of-stock products and receive an email notification when the product is back in stock.

== Description ==

AdMail is a powerful tool to help boost your sales and enhance customer experience. With AdMail, your customers can subscribe to out-of-stock products by providing their email address and receive an email notification when the product is back in stock.

AdMail is compatible with all WooCommerce product types, including simple, variable, and grouped products. You can customize the subscription form to appear as a popup, and choose from a variety of email templates. You can also enable/disable the subscription form for guests, provide an unsubscribe option for users, and enable/disable email submissions automatically.

AdMail prioritizes speed and performance, and your customers will enjoy faster performance thanks to the Ajax feature implemented.

== Installation ==

    Install AdMail from the WordPress plugins screen by searching for "AdMail".
    Activate AdMail through the "Plugins" screen in WordPress.
    Make sure WooCommerce is activated.

Alternatively, you can upload the plugin files to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory and activate AdMail through the "Plugins" screen in WordPress.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I install AdMail? =

To install AdMail, follow these steps:

    From your WordPress dashboard, go to Plugins.
    Click on "Add new" and search for "AdMail".
    Choose "AdMail - Back in Stock Notifier" and click install.
    After installation, click activate.

= Is AdMail compatible with all themes? =

AdMail is compatible with all themes that respect the coding standards of WordPress set by its Codex.

= Does AdMail work with custom single product templates? =

Yes, AdMail works with custom single product templates that use WooCommerce built-in hooks/filters/shortcodes.

= Will I have to send emails manually? =

No, AdMail can take care of the entire process, collecting subscriptions and sending emails to your customers when a product is back in stock. Simply enable the "Auto Submission" option in the plugin settings.

= Can I buy a custom email template? =

Yes, you can contact our team at admail@aleswebs.com to request a quote for a custom email template.

= Is AdMail compatible with all WooCommerce product types? =

Yes, AdMail is compatible with simple, variable, and grouped products.

= What is the difference between the free and pro versions of the AdMail plugin? =

The free version of AdMail enables your customers to subscribe to out-of-stock products and receive an email notification when the product is back in stock. The pro version of AdMail offers additional tools, such as sending promotional emails to inform your customers about new products and coupons.
